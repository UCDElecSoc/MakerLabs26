# Motors
Devices that convert electrical energy into mechanical motion. They are commonly used in robotics and automation.
## DC Motor
A motor that runs on direct current. It rotates continuously and is simple to control, often used for wheels and fans.

## Servo Motor

## Stepper Motor
A motor that moves in small, fixed steps. It allows very precise control of position and speed, often used in 3D printers and CNC machines.
